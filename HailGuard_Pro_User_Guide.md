# HailGuard Pro - User Guide

**Vehicle Hail Damage Intelligence Platform**  
for Touchless Auto Dent Removal Technology

**Version:** May 2026

---

## 1. Overview

HailGuard Pro is a professional intelligence tool built to help companies offering **touchless auto dent removal technology** identify high-potential markets, understand hail damage patterns, and find promising business partners or franchise locations.

### Key Capabilities

- View **30-year historical hail concentration** with an intensity slider
- Explore **specific 2026 severe hail events** using a date slider
- See **live recent vehicle hail damage reports**
- Identify **service gap areas** (high damage + low repair density)
- View **economic impact** and business ratings
- Get AI-powered **Region Intelligence** recommendations

**Best Use Case:** Sales demos, franchise development, and partner outreach in Tornado Alley and the Midwest.

---

## 2. Main Interface

The tool has three main parts:

### 1. Interactive Map (Center)
The main visual workspace. Pan and zoom freely. All data layers appear here.

### 2. Sidebar Controls (Left)
- Toggle layers on/off
- Adjust the **30-Year Intensity Slider**
- Adjust the **2026 Date Slider**

### 3. Region Intelligence Panel (Below the Map)
This is the most important feature for decision-making. It automatically updates based on where you are looking on the map and tells you:
- What data you’re seeing
- What it means for vehicle hail damage
- Whether the area is a good target for business development or franchising

---

## 3. Layer-by-Layer Guide

### 30-Year Hail Hotspots (Clean Professional View)
Shows long-term historical hail damage using clean circle markers instead of blurry heatmaps.

- **Slider:** “Minimum Hotspot Intensity”
- Drag right to show only the strongest historical zones
- Higher values = cleaner, more impactful visuals
- **Color Legend** (shown in sidebar):
  - Red = Very High
  - Orange = High
  - Yellow = Moderate-High
  - Green-Yellow = Moderate
  - Blue = Lower

### 2026 Severe Hail Events (Slider)
View specific severe hail events from 2026.

- Use the slider to move through different dates
- Only the selected event is shown on the map
- Excellent for showing recent, real-world damage examples
- Sidebar shows the currently selected event

### Other Layers

| Layer                        | What It Shows                              | Best Used For                     |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Recent Vehicle Hail Reports | Live data from last 24 hours               | Showing current activity          |
| Service Gaps                | Purple zones = High damage + Low service   | Franchise targeting               |
| Economic Impact             | Orange circles sized by annual damage $    | ROI prioritization                |
| Hail Repair Businesses      | Markers colored by rating                  | Finding partners                  |
| HRRR Risk Zones             | Areas with >80% hail probability           | Short-term risk awareness         |

## 4. Region Intelligence Panel (Key Decision Tool)

This panel updates automatically as you move the map.

**It answers three questions:**
1. What am I looking at right now?
2. What does this mean for vehicle hail damage?
3. Should I focus business development efforts here?

### Recommendation Types

- **Strongly Positive** — High damage + low service density → Excellent franchise or partnership opportunity
- **Positive (Competitive)** — High damage but more established repair shops
- **Positive (High Volume)** — Very high damage volume, good for high-throughput operations

**Pro Tip:** Zoom into “Strongly Positive” areas and turn on the Business layer to find high-rated shops that could be strong licensing or franchise partners.

---

## 5. Recommended Workflows

### For Franchise Development
1. Turn on **30-Year Hail Hotspots**
2. Increase the Intensity slider
3. Look for **“Strongly Positive”** in the Region Intelligence panel
4. Zoom in and turn on **Businesses** layer to find potential partners

### For Sales Demos
1. Start with the **2026 layer** and move the slider
2. Switch to **30-Year view** to show long-term patterns
3. Highlight **Service Gap** (purple) areas
4. Use the Region Intelligence panel to give data-backed recommendations

### For Internal Strategy
- Compare multiple regions by moving the map
- Use Economic Impact layer to prioritize highest-ROI markets

---

## 6. Tips & Best Practices

- The map updates live — pan and zoom freely during presentations
- Higher intensity slider values create cleaner visuals for executives
- The 2026 layer is powerful for showing “this is still happening now”
- City labels appear above the dots for easy orientation
- All data comes from free public sources (NOAA, NWS, IEM) + realistic modeling
