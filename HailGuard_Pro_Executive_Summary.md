# HailGuard Pro - Executive Summary

**One-Page Overview | May 2026**

---

## What is HailGuard Pro?

HailGuard Pro is a professional intelligence platform that helps companies offering **touchless auto dent removal technology** identify high-potential markets, understand hail damage patterns, and find strong franchise or partnership opportunities in Tornado Alley and the Midwest.

---

## Core Value

- **See where vehicle hail damage has been concentrated** over 30 years
- **View specific severe hail events from 2026** using an interactive date slider
- **Identify “Service Gap” zones** — areas with high historical damage but low repair business density
- **Get clear recommendations** on whether a region is a good target for business development

---

## Key Features

| Feature                        | What It Does                                      | Best For                              |
|--------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| 30-Year Hail Hotspots + Slider | Clean circle markers showing long-term damage     | Identifying strongest historical zones |
| 2026 Severe Hail Events Slider | View specific 2026 hail events by selecting dates | Showing recent, real damage examples   |
| Region Intelligence Panel      | Auto-updates with recommendations as you move map | Fast decision-making & presentations   |
| Service Gap Layer              | Highlights high-damage, low-service areas         | Franchise targeting                    |
| Business Ratings Layer         | Shows repair shops colored by star rating         | Finding potential partners             |

---

## How to Use It Effectively

**For Franchise Development**
- Turn on 30-Year Hotspots → Increase intensity slider → Look for “Strongly Positive” zones in the Region Intelligence panel.

**For Sales Demos**
- Start with the 2026 slider → Show recent events → Switch to 30-Year view → Highlight service gaps.

**For Strategy**
- Move the map and watch the Region Intelligence panel update in real time. Compare regions quickly.

---

## Why It Matters

Real user reports from X and Reddit confirm frequent vehicle dent claims and parking concerns during storms in these exact zones. HailGuard Pro turns that real-world pain into clear, visual, actionable intelligence for your team.
